Our Story

Celebrating talent, creativity, and cultural diversity through events and performances.

Anandutsav is the heart and soul of our college’s cultural celebrations, a festival that brings together talent, tradition, and togetherness. For the past 10 years, this grand event has been the biggest and most awaited festival on campus, setting new benchmarks of excitement and creativity every year.

From electrifying music and dance performances to mind-blowing art, drama, and literary events, Anandutsav is a platform where students showcase their skills and passion. The festival is a fusion of cultures, traditions, and modern expressions, making it an unforgettable experience for participants and audiences alike.

With each passing year, Anandutsav grows bigger and better, uniting students, faculty, and alumni in a vibrant celebration of art, culture, and creativity. Whether you’re here to compete, perform, or simply soak in the festive spirit, Anandutsav promises memories that last a lifetime!
